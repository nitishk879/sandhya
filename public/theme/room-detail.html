Spend A Tranquil Vacation in Manali

Sandhya Resort and Spa, Manali, is a luxurious resort enriched with world-class facilities and comfort. It is a stylish 4-star resort in Manali is ranking high in hospitality. The resort welcomes you for an indelible vacation in Manali. Experience the authentic flavour of a Himalayan holiday while we serve you with the best of amenities. Our luxury hotel in Manali is crafted in the traditional style with balconies offering breathtaking views. Sandhya Resort and Spa, Manali, is a gorgeous resort located on Kanyal Road inRangriDistrict, which takes thorough care of your comfort and convenience.

Our resort in Manali is strategically located close to Manali Bus Depot, offering ease of travel and transit to our guests. As one of the leading Manali Resorts, Sandhya Resort and Spa, Manali, is centrally located in the heart of the city offering easy access to some of the popular tourist attractions in Manali such as Hidimba Temple and Manali Bazar which make us an ideal choice for globetrotters who seek to explore the best that Manali offers.

Rooms

There are four types of rooms and suites at the hotel. These are- Deluxe Room, Luxury Room, Mountain Facing Room and 3 Bedroom Cottage Unit. All our rooms in Manali come with a balcony which offers a mesmerizing view. The mountain facing rooms look out at the Rohtang Pass.

Our resort in Manali has the most luxurious rooms with superior amenities which take care of your comfort while you stay with us.

Restaurant

Our Manali Resort houses an in-house multi-cuisine restaurant in Manali which offers cuisine from all around the world and features a wide spread of buffet from its live kitchen. Along with a scrumptious meal combined our restaurant offers an elegantly designed fine dining ambience and can accommodate 56 guests.
